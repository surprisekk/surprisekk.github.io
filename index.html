 Computer Science Club at ICFAI Tech School

Who We Are

The Computer Science Club at ICFAI Tech School is a student-run organization to encourage and strengthen community between students of computer science and other students and to spread information about computer science and technology through lectures, seminars, publications, and events.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to creating and maintaining a tight-knit community among Computer Science students and anyone interested in technology. Within this community, we aim to provide resources and opportunities for academic and career success, as well as an atmosphere for networking and making friends.
